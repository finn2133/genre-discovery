1. What is Genre Categorization?

Genre categorization is the process of organizing anime shows based on their themes, tone, or target audience. By using categories like action, romance, comedy, or slice-of-life, platforms make it easier for users to find content aligned with their interests.

  • Action: High-energy sequences, battles, and adventure stories.
  • Romance: Focused on relationships, emotional arcs, and love stories.
  • Comedy: Lighthearted shows designed to entertain and amuse.
  • Slice of Life: Everyday life stories that emphasize realism and character development.
  • Fantasy/Sci-Fi: Imaginative worlds, futuristic tech, or supernatural themes.
